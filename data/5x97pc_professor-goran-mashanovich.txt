Biography
About
Goran Mashanovich is a Professor of Group IV Photonics at the Optoelectronics Research Centre (ORC), Faculty of Engineering and Physical Sciences, University of Southampton, UK. He received Dipl. Ing. and MSc in Optoelectronics from the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, University of Belgrade, Serbia, and PhD in Silicon Photonics and MSc in innovative teaching from the University of Surrey, UK. He is head of the ORC Mid-IR silicon photonics group. His research interests include both passive and active devices in Si and Ge and their integration for communication and sensing applications. Prof. Mashanovich is author of 450 publications in the field of Silicon Photonics. Prof. Mashanovich is currently PI/Co-I on grants totalling >£20 million, awarded by EPSRC and industry. Goran is also a visiting professor at the Faculty of Electrical Engineering, University of Belgrade, Serbia.
