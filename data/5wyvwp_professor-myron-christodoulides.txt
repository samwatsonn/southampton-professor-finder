Biography
Biography
Myron Christodoulides is Professor of Bacteriology, with current research interests in the bacterial pathogens causing diseases in humans and animals. Projects are focused on development of vaccines, novel antimicrobials and rapid diagnostics with colleagues and collaborators globally. Myron is also an Academy of Medical Sciences Hamied Hamied Foundation UK-India AMR Visiting Professor.
