Biography
Biography
Current post:     2007 – present: Professor of Dermatology, Dermatopharmacology, University of Southampton / Honorary Consultant Dermatologist, University Hospital Southampton NHS Foundation Trust, UK.Qualifications:BA, MB BCh BAO (Hons);  Medicine, Trinity College Dublin, Ireland (1987).MRCPI;  Royal College of Physicians of Ireland (1989).CCST Dermatology;  Royal College of Physicians, London, UK (1997).PhD;  Newcastle University, Newcastle upon Tyne, UK (1998).FRCP;  Royal College of Physicians of London, UK (2004).FRSB;  Royal Society of Biology, UK (2011).FAoP;  Association of Physicians of Great Britain and Ireland (member from 2002; fellow from 2021).Previous posts (reverse chronological order):2001 – 2006: Medical Research Council Senior Clinical Fellow / Professor of Molecular Dermatology (from 2002) / Honorary Consultant Dermatologist, Dermatopharmacology, University of Southampton, UK.1999 – 2001 Medical Research Council Clinician Scientist Fellow / Senior Lecturer / Honorary Consultant Dermatologist, Dermatopharmacology, University of Southampton, UK.1997 – 1999 Medical Research Council Clinician Scientist Fellow / Honorary Consultant (from 1998), Dermatology, University of Newcastle upon Tyne, & MRC Human Genetics Unit, Edinburgh, UK.1994 – 1997 Medical Research Council Clinical Training Fellow / Honorary Senior Registrar, Dermatology, University of Newcastle upon Tyne, UK.1993 – 1994 Clinical Lecturer / Honorary Registrar, Dermatology, University of Newcastle upon Tyne, UK.1991 – 1993 Registrar in Dermatology, City of Dublin Skin & Cancer Hospital and St. Vincent’s University Hospital, Dublin, Ireland.1987 – 1991 General Professional Medical Training, Federated Dublin Voluntary Hospitals & Beaumont University Hospital, Ireland.
